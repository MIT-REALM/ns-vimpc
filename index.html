Neural Shield Variational Inference Model Predictive Control (VIMPC)

Offline, we collect a dataset and train a NN approximation \(B_\theta\) of a DCBF. Online, we modify the VIMPC architecture to use resampling-based rollouts to improve the sampling distribution of the Monte Carlo estimator in the presence of the DCBF state constraints. We also integrate the DCBF safety condition into the optimization objective function.

Inspired by particle filter, Resampling-based Rollouts (RBR) concentrates all sampled trajectories in safe region

Related Work

This work is part of our line of work on designing safe and efficient Variational Inference MPC. Other works on this line includes:

CC-MPPI (ICRA 2022): Using covariance steering to improve the sample efficiency for MPPI.
RA-MPPI (ICRA 2023): A sampling-based MPC that accounts for arbitrary forms of dynamical and environmental disturbances.
Shield-MPPI (RA-L): Achieving safety while significantly saves computational resources for sampling-based MPC by integrating an approximate control barrier function.
BSS-MPPI (CDC 2024): Extending the Shield-MPPI to a more general setting with belief-space dynamics.
For a survey of the field of learning safe control for multi-robot systems, see this paper.
